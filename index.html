The artist

Meet Tamanna

Tamanna has spent years perfecting the quiet, patient art of henna — the kind that shows up neat and clear the moment it's drawn, and deep and dark days later. Clients return to her again and again, from birthdays to bridal mornings, because the work is precise, the henna is organic, and the experience feels personal from the first cone to the last detail.

She works from a reference with exact precision when you have a design in mind, and free-hand with just as much confidence when you don't — bringing warmth, punctuality and genuine care to every appointment.
